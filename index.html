About Me

I am Sreeraj Dabbiru, an undergraduate pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science and Systems Engineering at LIET, Class of 2028. Alongside my academic pursuits, I hold the position of Editor-In-Chief at LWC, where I combine leadership with strategic communication to drive meaningful engagement.

Proficient in languages such as C, Python, HTML, and CSS, I am steadily advancing toward full-stack development expertise and expanding my knowledge in Machine Learning. I view technology as a vital tool for addressing complex real-world challenges, with a focus on crafting intuitive and impactful digital solutions.

Beyond my technical endeavors, I am a recognized winner of the District Poetry Competition – Yuva Utsav 2024. This accomplishment underscores my unique ability to blend technical precision with creative expression, a balance I intentionally cultivate in all forms of communication.

As a class representative, I prioritize collaboration, leadership, and empathy, striving to foster environments of mutual growth. My commitment is to continuous learning, personal evolution, and meaningful contribution within every community I engage.
